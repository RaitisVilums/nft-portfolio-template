LazyMinersClub is a community build for NFT art and Cryptocurrency mining enthusiasts. Our team has decided to offer our unique NFT art in our quest to seek for motivated investors looking for financial freedom.

About
LazyMinersClub

Beginner friendly

By purchasing our NFT's you invest in a real LMC GPU hardware, which we will install in our mining facility, mine the crypto for you, handle all the electricity expenses, maintenance and do weekly payouts to your NFT crypto address.

Valuable asset

Acquiring our NFT art not only will earn passive income for you by your unique LMC GPU hardware, but also grants you access to exclusive private discord, early releases, private NFT holder events, giveaways & more.

Community

LMC is professional and friendly community built for everyone. Joining our community is not only profitable decision, but also a very fun experience. We love to interact with our community and host some online events.

Road Map

  10. Phase 10: Improvement. Switching to wind powered electricity and setting up backup generators.
